Further Help

Using OmniDazzle with Apple Keynote:

  • To enable the use of OmniDazzle on top of a Keynote presentation, you should open Keynote's Slide Show preferences and enable the Allow Expose, Dashboard and others to use the screen setting.

If you need some more help, you have several options.

  • The OmniDazzle web site is always a good place to look for the latest news.
  • The Omni Group Forums are a fine place to meet Omni staff and users to talk about all things Omni.
  • The Send Feedback command in the Help menu is a great way to get in touch with a human being who will read and reply to your message. Send us your comments, questions, ideas, epic poetry, et cetera.

Menus

In the OmniDazzle menu: Choose Check for Updates to see whether there is a new version of OmniDazzle available online. Choose Software Update Preferences to control whether OmniDazzle should check for new versions automatically.

In the Window menu: Choose Configure to summon the OmniDazzle window.

In the Help menu: Choose OmniDazzle Help to summon the thing you're reading right now. Choose Send Feedback to create an e-mail aimed at the OmniDazzle support folks.

Menu Bar Extra

The menu bar extra appears when you choose Show Controls in Menu Bar from the OmniDazzle menu. Click it for a menu with commands to open the OmniDazzle window, to quit OmniDazzle, or to quickly switch to a different plug-in.

Dock Icon

Click and hold on OmniDazzle's icon in the Dock for a menu you can use to quickly switch to a different plug-in.

Plug-ins

The top of the OmniDazzle window has a 3-D view of the available effect plug-ins. Each card has a live preview using the effect's current settings. In the center is the currently selected plug-in. To select a different effect, just click one of the other cards.

Some plug-ins may not appear, based on the capabilities of your graphics hardware. We've tried to accomodate a wide range of configurations, but some plug-ins require features not available on all hardware.

Each plug-in has its own info, commands, and settings. Click the Info tab to see a description of the effect and a list of its commands. Click the Configuration tab to change the commands for activating the effect, or to adjust the settings for how the effect looks.
